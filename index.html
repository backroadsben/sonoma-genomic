DNA technology

DNA contains the blueprint for every organism and the unique signatures that differentiate. A double helix is formed through the binding of two strands—a remarkable recognition event utilized in epidemiology, forensics, diagnostics, food safety, and environmental monitoring. All depend on DNA in the form of oligonucleotides—short fragments that are chemically synthesized and often fluorescent modified.
